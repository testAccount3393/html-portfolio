The Best Movies According to Alexander Dalessi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Terminator

Iconic movie from the 1980s featuring California's former governor.

It's A Wonderful Life

The greatest Christmas movie ever made. It still holds up in 2023.

Pulp Fiction

Violent at times but iconic and fantastic movie. Known knowns and known unknows and unknown unknowns. Things we don't know we don't know.
